Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: September 16, 2025

Overview

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for eligible products. Items must be unused, with original tags and packaging.

How to return

  1. Contact support@kanyaraag.example with order number and reason.
  2. We will provide a return authorization and instructions.
  3. Ship the item back within the given timeframe.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the item, we will process the refund within 5-7 business days. Shipping charges may not be refundable.

Non-returnable items

Certain items such as intimate wear or final sale products cannot be returned unless defective.
